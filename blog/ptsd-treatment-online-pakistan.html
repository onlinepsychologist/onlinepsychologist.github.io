Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) can make you feel stuck in the past. Online PTSD treatment offers a safe, supportive way to process trauma and reclaim your life — from anywhere in Pakistan.

Understanding PTSD

PTSD can develop after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. Symptoms include flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, and hypervigilance. These symptoms are treatable with professional help.

Evidence-Based Approaches

I use trauma-informed approaches including CBT and client-centered therapy to help you process traumatic experiences at your own pace.

Signs You May Have PTSD

  • Intrusive memories or flashbacks of a traumatic event
  • Avoiding places or situations that remind you of the trauma
  • Negative changes in mood and thinking
  • Feeling constantly on edge or easily startled
  • Difficulty sleeping or concentrating
